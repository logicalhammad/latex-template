\section{Software Deployment}

The deployment chapter explains how the developed system is prepared, configured, and made available for use in its intended environment. It covers the steps, tools, and platforms required to deploy the system, ensuring that it runs as expected outside the development setting. This may include details on hosting (local server, cloud, or on-premises), installation procedures, configuration settings, and dependencies required for successful execution. The chapter also highlights any deployment challenges, environment requirements (hardware/software), and guidelines for future scalability and maintenance.
